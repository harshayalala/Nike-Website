WebAbout

Nike, Inc. is a global leader in the design, development, and marketing of athletic footwear, apparel, and equipment. Established in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, it officially became Nike, Inc. in 1971. The iconic Swoosh logo, representing motion and speed, has become one of the most recognizable symbols in the world. Nike's mission is to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete* in the world (*if you have a body, you are an athlete). From groundbreaking shoe technology to impactful marketing campaigns, Nike continuously pushes the boundaries of sports and culture. Their commitment to sustainability and community initiatives further solidifies their position as a forward-thinking industry leader. Whether you're a professional athlete or someone who enjoys an active lifestyle, Nike provides high-quality products designed to enhance performance and style. Join the movement and discover why Nike remains at the forefront of athletic excellence.
